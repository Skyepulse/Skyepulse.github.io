A Unity team project

Ecolife is a game developed by a team of 6 students using Unity as a school project. In this game you play as a fox that discovers his environment and the many man-made threats that endanger it.

Discover the changes

The impact of the humans are revealed to the player as he progresses through the checkpoints. When hunting, a road will appear making it harder to catch preys, a hunter will also appear to exterminate the foxes, and the forest will burn down with fireplaces.

My contribution

I was on the backend and frontend teams. I worked on the whole fox move set and overall controller feel, as well as the hunter. I also implemented the agents AI system (rabbits, hunters, foxes, etc.) for map movement and interaction. I implemented one of the two mini-games in the game, the hunting mini-game.

Results

The game was well received by the students, and we won the prize of best game of the year from our school. We made a presentation stand where people could come and play the game.
